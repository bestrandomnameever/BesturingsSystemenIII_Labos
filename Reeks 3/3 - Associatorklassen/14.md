#### Wat is er bijzonder aan de verbinding gerealiseerd door de associatorklasse Win32_DependentService? Wat stelt de verbinding voor ?

= represents an association between two interdependent base services.

#### Wat is de betekenis van het extra attribuut? Is dit ingevuld voor bepaalde instanties?

TypeOfDependency = The nature of the service to service dependency. This property describes that the associated service must have completed (value=2), must be started (3) or must not be started (4) in order for the service to function.
